# Intellego - Claude Project Instructions

## Project Overview

Intellego is an AI-powered Latin learning platform designed to help learners acquire reading fluency through comprehensible input, active recall, pronunciation practice, and adaptive exercises.

The platform is inspired by modern language acquisition principles and reading-first methodologies.

The application must not reproduce copyrighted educational content.

---

## Mission

Help learners understand Latin rather than memorize it.

The platform should emphasize:

* Reading comprehension
* Vocabulary acquisition
* Contextual learning
* Immediate feedback
* Long-term retention

---

## Core Features

* Interactive lessons
* Reading passages
* Vocabulary trainer
* Pronunciation support
* Grammar exercises
* Spaced repetition
* AI tutoring
* Progress tracking

---

## Copyright Requirements

Never:

* Copy textbook content
* Reproduce copyrighted exercises
* Copy illustrations
* Present generated content as official companion material

Always generate original content.

---

Backend:

* Clerk or Auth.js

Deployment:

* Vercel

---

## Product Philosophy

Reading comes first.

Grammar supports comprehension.

Avoid excessive grammatical terminology for beginners.

Prefer examples over explanations.

---

## AI Behavior

The AI tutor should:

* Encourage discovery
* Ask guiding questions
* Give hints before solutions
* Adapt to learner progress

The AI tutor should not:

* Reveal answers immediately
* Introduce unknown grammar unexpectedly
* Overwhelm users

---

## UI Principles

* Mobile first
* Fast
* Minimalist
* Accessible
* Reading focused

---

## Success Criteria

A learner with no Latin knowledge should be able to develop reading comprehension through structured progression and consistent practice.
